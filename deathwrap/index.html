Deathwrap

DJ SET

Deathwrap is the alias of Nerea, a Galician DJ emerging as a dynamic presence within Spain’s underground electronic music scene. With two years of experience behind the decks, she has already developed a distinct identity rooted in high-speed blends, bold selections, and an unapologetically raw approach to sound.

With an ear tuned to the pulse of the scene and a clear vision shaped by rebellion and rhythm, Deathwrap continues to evolve as one of the promising new voices of Spain’s next wave of electronic artists.

Biography

Her musical journey began with explorations in Industrial and Hard Techno, which laid the foundation for her evolving style. Over time, she gravitated toward a more rhythmically explosive and emotionally charged fusion of Hard Bounce, Latin Club, and Hard Trance, a combination that now defines her signature sound. Her sets are fast, driving, and intensely physical, crafted to ignite unconventional dancefloors and push sonic boundaries.

As a freelance artist, Deathwrap has made her mark across local raves and independent events in Galicia, where her high-energy performances have gained a loyal following. She has also brought her sound to prominent venues, including Hyper Club and Mondo Club in Vigo, where she made the warm up for Virtual Diva, one of Spain’s most visible figures in the crossover between digital culture and club music. She also appeared at Octogon (Madrid) as part of the Hard Bounz showcase, expanding her reach to a national audience.

Her style is marked by rapid transitions, rhythmic tension, and an instinct for selection that reflects both cultural roots and underground spirit. Influenced by the vibrant intersections between Latin rhythm and European rave aesthetics, Deathwrap stands out as an artist unafraid to blur genre lines and provoke movement.
