6EJOU

LIVE
6EJOU burst onto the scene in 2019 with his hypnotic industrial techno productions. Today, he tours globally with all-hardware live sets. His label Industrial Violence, launched in 2020, supports underground talents worldwide.

Biography

Known for his harsh and dark sound, 6EJOU's productions are infused with industrial murk yet maintain rhythm and dub-space. His sets blur the lines between DJ and live performance, using cutting-edge tools and creativity.
