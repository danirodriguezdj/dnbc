DANI RODRIGUEZ

DJ SET
Dani Rodríguez is a DJ and producer from Redondela, Galicia, active in the techno scene since 2019. Specializing in schranz and industrial, he has crafted a fast, relentless style designed to channel raw energy and connect soul-to-soul with the audience. For Dani, being a DJ means letting his spirit speak through the speakers.

Biography

Since 2020, he’s been producing his own music, with releases on Eclipse Recordings and self-released tracks across all major streaming platforms. His explosive energy has led him to perform in some of the most respected clubs across Europe: Nexus (Paris, France), STK Warehouse (Krakow, Poland), The Garage of The Bass Valley (Barcelona, Spain), E1 (London, UK), Fire (London, UK), Queen (Vigo, Spain), Pyramid (Monçao, Portugal), Riktus (Lisbon, Portugal), The Wall Club (Naples, Italy), Mantra (Genoa, Italy), and Trax Club (Vigo, Spain) among others.

He is the founder of Die Nacht Brennt Club, a concept that goes beyond a collective, it’s a way of life, currently evolving into an agency, a record label, and a new generation of techno events.
