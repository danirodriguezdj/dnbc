6EJOU

LIVE
6EJOU burst onto the scene in 2019 with his hypnotic industrial techno productions, captivating many along the way. Today, he tours globally, delivering powerful all-hardware live sets. His own label, Industrial Violence, launched in 2020 to offer opportunities to talented artists struggling to share their sound with the world. Ready to obliterate your dancefloor, 6EJOU’s debut EP is regarded by many as seminal, launching his career and creating a ripple of excitement through the techno community.

Biography

Known for his harsh and dark sound, 6EJOU’s productions are infused with industrial murk, yet maintain a sophisticated sense of rhythm and dub-space. His sets blur the lines between DJ and live performances, using cutting-edge hardware and software solutions. With his multi-functional production skills and inventive DJ techniques, 6EJOU proves that hard techno can also be smart, sensuous, and danceable.
